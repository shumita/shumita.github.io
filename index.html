ABOUT ME

ABOUT ME

Artist, Illustrator, Toy Designer, Graphic Designer

Hello! I'm Shumita Littlefield, a 22 year old recent graduate from Boston University. I love creating art, whether it be sketching, painting, sculpting, or sewing.

At Boston University, I studied Child Psychology and Visual Arts, with a focus in illustration and graphic design. I am proficient in programs such as Adobe CC, Procreate, Figma, and Canva. Some of my other skills include working with children, customer service, organization skills, time management, teamwork, and attention to detail.

Since 2021, I have been the Head of Creative Design and Illustration at sustainability startup company Idori. I created all art and visual assets associated with the brand, including picture book illustration, website graphics, computer game sprites, and merchandise concept art.

During my time at BU, I was an Undergraduate Research Assistant for the Developing Minds project at the Boston University Child Cognition Lab. My focus was on the Evolving Minds Project, lead by Dr. Deborah Kelemen. I created both original illustrations, as well as updated old art used in the curriculum.
