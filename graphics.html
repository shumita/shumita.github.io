ABOUT ME

Aspiring Graphic Designer

Hello! I'm Shumita Littlefield, a 22 year old senior at Boston University. I grew up in Newton, Massachusetts, a suburb outside of Boston. I love creating art, whether it be sketching, painting, sculpting, or sewing.

I study Visual Arts with a focus in illustration and graphic design, and Psychology with a focus in developmental and child psychology. Currently, I am an Undergrad Research Assistant for the Developming Minds project at the Boston University Child Cognition Lab, as well as the illustrator for start-up company Idori.

I have skills in drawing/illustrating, sewing, sculpting, and graphic design. I have experience in Procreate, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Premiere Pro. Some of my other skills include working with children, customer service, organization skills, time management, teamwork, and attention to detail.

Shumita Littlefield


I am the Head of Creative Design and Illustration for Idori, a company founded by CEO Noah Sorin at the BU Questrom School of Business. The company's goal is to teach children about sustainability, and create an impact that promotes more earth-friendly living. Idori aims to do this through stuffed animals and picture books containing stories about various environmental dangers. So far three picture books are in the works, and the first book is reaching its release date.

I am also an undergraduate research assistant at the Child Cognition Lab at Boston University. I am working on the Evolving Minds Project, lead by Professor Deborah Kelemen. In my current work, I illustrate pictures of animals to aid children in imagining the effects of evolution.
