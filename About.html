About Me


General

Hello! I'm Shumita Littlefield, a 20 year old student at Boston University. I am a psychology major and a visual arts minor. I grew up in Newton, Massachusetts, a suburb outside of Boston. I enjoy doing art, whether it be sketching, painting, or sculpting. I am currently illustrating a series of picture books about sustainability.


Interests

In addition to art, I love studying abnormal psychology, as well as developmental psychology. In my free time I love reading, watching TV, listening to music, and collecting small toys.

Skills

I have skills in drawing, sewing, sculpting, and graphic design. In the past I've used my skills to design posters, t-shirts, stickers, and stationary. Some of my work can be seen on LinkedIn. I have experience using:

  1. Adobe Illustrator
  2. Procreate, Photoshop
  3. Premiere Pro
  4. and InDesign.
