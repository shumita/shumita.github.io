ABOUT ME

Aspiring Toy Designer

Hello! I'm Shumita Littlefield, a 21 year old senior at Boston University. I love creating art, whether it be sketching, painting, sculpting, or sewing. I have a passion for toys and want to design products for a toy company in the future.

I am a Psychology Major, focusing on developmental and children's psychology. I am also a Visual Arts Minor, with a focus in illustration and graphic design. Currently, I am an Undergrad Research Assistant for the Developming Minds project at the Boston University Child Cognition Lab, as well as the illustrator for the start-up company, Idori, formed by a fellow BU Student.

I have skills in drawing/illustrating, sewing, sculpting, and graphic design. I have experience in Procreate, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Premiere Pro. Some of my other skills include working with children, customer service, organization skills, time management, teamwork, and attention to detail.

In the future, I hope to create toys that speak to the diversity in our world. For me, the best toy brands are the ones that are easy for all kinds of people to connect with, and to spark their creativity and imagination. I hope be involved with the toy design process from start to finish, whether that be conceptualizing a toy, deciding on the materials, or focus group testing.

I am currently an undergraduate research assistant at the Child Cognition Lab at Boston University. I am working on the Evolving Minds Project, lead by Professor Deborah Kelemen. In my current work, I illustrate pictures of animals to aid children in imagining the effects of evolution.

I also illustrate for Idori, a company founded by Noah Sorin at the BU Questrom School of Business. The company's goal is to teach children about sustainability, and create an impact that promotes more earth-friendly living. Idori aims to do this through stuffed animals and picture books containing stories about various environmental dangers. So far three picture books have been planned, and the first book is reaching its release date.
