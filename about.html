ABOUT ME


I'm Shumita Littlefield, a recent graduate from Boston University. I studied Child Psychology and Visual Arts, and I hope to go into the toy design, book illustration, or other children's media industry.

Storytelling, illustration and design are my passions, and I have devoted myself to cultivating the skills and experiences that will allow me to grow and thrive as a creative visual artist and designer. My experience in numerous design coding languages such as HTML5 and CSS, as well as programs such as Adobe CC, Microsoft Office, AutoCAD, Canva, and Procreate have allowed me to design website layouts, create detailed illustrations for children’s books, and develop captivating communications assets.

As Head of Creative Design and Illustration at Idori, I solely led and developed all the startup’s branding from concept to final production, such as the creation of their logo, marketing emails, website, and interactive digital games. The major component of my work was leading the illustration and development of all visual components of 3 complete picture books, including typography and text placement.

In my time at Boston University, I have worked with Dr. Deborah Keleman on her Evolving Minds Project to update the lesson illustrations, student workbooks, and create posters, brochures, flyers, and slide decks, all focused on teaching children across the United States about evolution and natural selection in animal populations. This essential project afforded me the opportunity to collaborate with an interdisciplinary team of scientists and educators from diverse backgrounds.

Feel free to contact me should you have any questions about my work, and I welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience can contribute to your projects.
